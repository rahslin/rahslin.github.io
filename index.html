About Me

Nine years of shipping great software

A bit of background on who I am, how I work, and what I care about.

I'm Rahslin, a full stack developer with 9 years of professional experience building web applications across a wide range of industries. I've worked with startups, agencies, and established companies to design and deliver solutions that are both technically sound and user-focused.

On the frontend, I specialize in React and modern JavaScript ecosystems. On the backend, I build robust APIs with Node.js and Python, backed by relational and NoSQL databases. I have strong experience with cloud infrastructure, CI/CD pipelines, and DevOps practices.

I believe in writing code that's readable, tested, and maintainable — not just code that works today. I enjoy mentoring junior developers and collaborating closely with designers and product teams.

Outside of work, I enjoy exploring new technologies, contributing to open source, and occasionally writing about engineering topics.
